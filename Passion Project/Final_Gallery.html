Ohio University Music Industry Summit
Commissioned for the 2022 Ohio University Music Industry Summit. The goal of this mural was to welcome the keynote speakers which included Noelle Scaggs, SABA, Michell Zauner of Japaneese Breakfast, and CAAMP. The conference print and web materials had specific branding so that style was incorporated for this mural.
Ukraine
Not only do I do murals for events, but I do them for causes. I am a student in the Master of Fine Arts: Communication Media Arts program. Two of my friends in the program, Les and Olena are from Ukraine. When the war started I wanted to raise awareness whithin our community in Athens and stand in solidarity with my friends. This mural is not technical but rather it was a way for Olena and myself to express the sadness and helplessness that was felt early on.
Superbowl LVI
Ohio Unviersity commissioned me for this project. Athens, Ohio is the home of Joe Burrow, the quarterback of the Cincinatti Bengals which played in the 2022 Superbowl. This was a three part mural with the main focus being the portrait of Joe wearing the infamous fleece jacket and JB9 chain.
Homecoming 2021
Ohio University Homecoming Week is always a special event. Many alumni from all over come back to Athens to reunite and celebrate the weekend with many festivities. On Saturday there is a parade followed by a football game. This mural was created to be used as a photo wall for those passing by either heading to the parade or to the football game.
Welcome Home Bobcats
This was another mural commissioned by Ohio Unviersity for Welcome Weekend. Students were moving to Athens, Ohio for the first time since the pandemic. The grafitti wall is a place where students can come and paint anything that they want either for fun or for a cause, but it can seem intimidating. So, we hosted an event where students could come and contribute to the wall and leave their first mark at Ohio University.
